Privacy Policy

Thiis Privacy Policy explains how AKC Tax Calculator (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects user information. This app is owned and operated by Anurag Karundia & Co.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide accurate tax calculation functionality, AKC Tax Calculator requires users to manually enter the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • PAN Number

This information is used only for calculating tax within the App. The App does not require any user registration, login, or account creation.

2. Data Storage and Sharing

AKC Tax Calculator does not store user data on any external server. All information entered by users is processed locally within the App and is not shared with any third party. The App does not sell, trade, or rent user information to anyone.

3. Third-Party Services

AKC Tax Calculator does not use any third-party services, including analytics tools or advertising networks.

4. Advertisements

The App does not display advertisements and does not use any ad-related services.

5. Data Security

AKC Tax Calculator values user privacy and security. Since the App does not store or transmit user data externally, the risk of unauthorized access or misuse of personal information is minimized.

6. Children’s Privacy

AKC Tax Calculator is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. User Consent

By using AKC Tax Calculator and entering the required information, users agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Users are advised to review this page periodically for updates.
